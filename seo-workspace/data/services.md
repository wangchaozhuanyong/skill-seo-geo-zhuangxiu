## Shared process

1. Consultation: understand goals, space, style, and budget.
2. Site Measurement: inspect the site and confirm key dimensions.
3. Design Proposal: prepare layout ideas and visual direction where relevant.
4. Quotation & Material Selection: provide a clear breakdown and discuss materials.
5. Construction: manage work with site supervision.
6. Handover: final quality check and project delivery.
