# Content Quality Policy

Good renovation SEO/GEO content should include:

- specific service details
- real service area
- customer problems
- process explanation
- budget factors, not fake fixed prices
- timeline factors, not fake completion promises
- material choices and trade-offs
- real case proof when available
- clearly labeled design concepts or planning examples when real proof is unavailable
- FAQ
- internal links
- CTA
- image brief and alt text
- schema recommendation
- owner review notes

Prefer improving high-intent service pages and existing useful pages over producing thin new articles.

Every draft/report should be useful to a real renovation customer, not only to search engines.
