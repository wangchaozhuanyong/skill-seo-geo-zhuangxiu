# Anti-Spam Policy

Never create:

- fake reviews or testimonials
- fake case studies
- fake before/after proof
- fake awards, media mentions, certifications, licenses, insurance, or supplier partnerships
- fake prices, fixed budgets, fixed timelines, guarantees, discounts, or warranty terms
- unsupported service areas
- doorway pages
- city-swap duplicate pages
- mass query-variation pages
- AI bait pages created only to manipulate AI summaries
- keyword-stuffed copy
- copied competitor content

Allowed when clearly labeled:

- concept design
- rendering concept
- effect-rendering direction
- planning example
- material mood board
- layout idea
- scenario-based guidance

Labels must make clear that the content is planning material, not a completed real project.
