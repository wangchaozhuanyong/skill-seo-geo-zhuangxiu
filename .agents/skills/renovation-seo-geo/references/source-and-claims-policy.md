# Source and Claims Policy

## Allowed claims

Use claims supported by:

- Business owner-provided facts
- Existing website content
- Real case studies
- Real customer reviews
- Real photos
- Public company profiles
- Reliable external sources where needed

## Allowed design-planning material

The owner allows renovation content to use non-case-study material when it is useful for customers and clearly labeled, including:

- Effect renderings
- 3D visualization descriptions
- Design concepts
- Layout planning examples
- Material and finish guidance
- Room-by-room renovation ideas
- Scenario-based planning examples

Use labels such as "设计方案", "效果图方案", "概念设计", "规划示例", "参考方案", "rendering concept", or "design concept".

These are allowed as content planning assets, but they are not factual proof. Do not describe them as completed real projects, real customer cases, before/after results, customer reviews, confirmed prices, fixed timelines, or warranty promises.

## Claims requiring confirmation

Mark these as NEEDS OWNER INPUT unless already verified:

- Exact prices
- Exact timelines
- Warranty terms
- Licenses
- Insurance
- Certifications
- Awards
- Number of completed projects
- Years in business
- Staff credentials
- Supplier partnerships
- Customer testimonials
- Before/after results
- Service areas
- Emergency availability
- Permit/building-code statements

Do not mark a draft as blocked only because the owner has not provided real cases, real photos, fixed prices, fixed timelines, warranty terms, or customer reviews. Continue with clearly labeled design-planning material and avoid unsupported factual claims.

## Never create

- Fake testimonials
- Fake project examples
- Fake customer names
- Fake ratings
- Fake awards
- Fake local offices
- Fake "near me" pages
- Fake guarantees
- Fake discounts
- Fake scarcity
- Concept/rendering examples presented as completed real projects

## External sources

Use external sources only for general educational information, such as:

- search engine documentation
- official building or safety guidance
- reputable manufacturer documentation
- government or local authority guidance

Do not use competitor websites as copy sources.
