# Google Ads Official Learning

Use this reference when refreshing PPC guidance or learning platform behavior. Prefer official Google documentation over third-party advice for product behavior, certification paths, and policy-sensitive decisions.

## Learning Sources

- Skillshop: https://skillshop.withgoogle.com/
- Google Ads Skillshop: https://skillshop.withgoogle.com/googleads
- Google Ads training on Skillshop: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/7539883
- Google Ads certifications: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/9702955

Prioritize these learning paths for this renovation PPC skill:

1. Google Ads Search
2. Google Ads Measurement
3. Google Analytics / conversion measurement
4. Performance Max only after Search and measurement are stable
5. Video, Display, Shopping, or Apps only when the business case is explicit

## Refresh Rules

- Recheck official docs when Google Ads UI wording, AI Max, recommendation behavior, conversion setup, policy status, or budget pacing appears different from the local playbook.
- Summarize official guidance in local reports; do not paste long copyrighted passages.
- Record learning updates with `latest-research` only when the source is actually selected and the claim boundary is clear.
- Do not use Skillshop certification language to claim the business is certified, a Google Partner, or guaranteed to perform unless owner-verified proof exists.
